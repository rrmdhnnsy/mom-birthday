Dear Mom,

There’s honestly so much I want to write to you, but I think I should start with how grateful I am.

Thank you so much for always being there for me, even when it’s hard. Thank you for being the anchor to my ship, the one who grounds me when I start to spiral with my insecurities and tendency to overthink.

We’re so much alike, and maybe that’s a little scary to think about. But maybe, because of that, I feel like I can understand you, even when nothing is said out loud. But who knows, maybe that’s just my overthinking again. After all, I know I can never fully understand exactly how you feel.

But just in case, I want you to know this. Your presence matters so much to us, your family. We love and care about you so, so, sooo much, even if we don’t say it enough. Without you, our lives wouldn’t be the same. Mine wouldn’t be the same. It would be so much emptier without you in it.

You’ve told me so many times that I’m a miracle given by god to you, but I still can’t fully comprehend that. Because to me, you are the miracle. You are the one who is strong enough to endure everything and still laugh and smile at me. You are the one who still believes in me, even after all of my breakdowns and worries..

I know there are things in your life that have been weighing on you, and I’m sorry I haven’t always noticed or given you the comfort you deserve.

There’s still so much I don’t know about you or your life, but one thing I know is this: You are my home and my comfort.

I’m sorry I can’t just say this out loud to you, because I know I would end up choking on my words as I read it aloud and end up a crying mess.

Today, let’s celebrate you, your life, your strength, everything that makes you *you*. May your days be filled with light and happiness.

Happy birthday to the heart of the family.

With so much love,
Syifa ❤︎
